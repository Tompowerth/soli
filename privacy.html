Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Our Promise

SOLI handles the most personal and sensitive information imaginable — your emotions, your thoughts, your face, your voice. We take this responsibility seriously. Your data belongs to you. Period.

7 Privacy Commitments

  • Your conversations are yours. No human at SOLI reads your conversations. Ever.
  • Face images are never stored. Camera analysis happens in real-time and is immediately discarded. We do not save, store, or transmit images of your face.
  • Voice is never recorded. We analyze emotional metadata (tone, pace, stress level) but we never record or store your actual voice.
  • Your data is never sold. Not to advertisers, not to data brokers, not to anyone. Ever.
  • Your data does not train AI models. Your conversations are not used to train or improve any AI system.
  • Full deletion in one click. You can permanently delete all your data at any time from the Settings menu. When you delete, it's gone. No backups, no archives.
  • Military-grade encryption. All data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.

What We Collect

We collect only what's needed to provide SOLI's service:

  • Account info: Email, name, and birth date (for zodiac features).
  • Conversations: Text messages between you and SOLI, stored encrypted in your personal account.
  • Emotional metadata: Aggregate emotional patterns (e.g., "user was stressed on Tuesday") — never raw audio or images.
  • Usage data: Session duration and feature usage for service improvement.

What We Never Collect

  • Photos or images of your face
  • Voice recordings
  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone data
  • Data from other apps

Data Security

All data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and transmitted over TLS 1.3. Our database uses Row Level Security (RLS) — each user's data is isolated and inaccessible to other users or SOLI staff. We conduct regular security audits.

Third Parties

We use the following services to operate SOLI:

  • Google (Gemini AI): Processes conversations. Subject to Google's AI data usage policies. We send only the current conversation — no personal identifiers.
  • Supabase: Database hosting (Frankfurt, EU). ISO 27001 certified.
  • Vercel: Application hosting.
  • Stripe: Payment processing. SOLI never sees your credit card number.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data
  • Delete all your data permanently
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Children

SOLI is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
